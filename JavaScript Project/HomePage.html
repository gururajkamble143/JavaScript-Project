What is Expanding Universe?

Expanding Universe is a free, web-based collection of digital interactives and videos highlighting the latest developments
in astronomy and Earth science.

Expanding Universe gives you the opportunity to explore our planet, solar system, galaxy, and universe. Provided free with the support
of NASA, Expanding Universe is developed by a team of scientists, educators, and communication specialists who collaborate to ensure
that content is accurate, up-to-date, engaging, relevant, and accessible to a wide audience.
